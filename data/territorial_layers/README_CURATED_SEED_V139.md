# v139 curated seed dataset

This dataset provides the first curated real territorial records for vet.ector's `/territorial-layers` endpoint.

## Included records

- West Nile virus, Caserta, 2026
- West Nile virus, Firenze, 2026
- West Nile virus, Verona, 2026

## Source basis

The seed is derived from ECDC weekly surveillance for week 27, produced on 2 July 2026 with data submitted up to 1 July 2026, which listed Italy with confirmed locally acquired human WNV cases in Caserta, Firenze and Verona. The Centro Nazionale Sangue West Nile Virus 2026 page, updated on 13 July 2026, also lists preventive measures/circulation monitoring notices for Italian provinces including Caserta, Firenze and Verona.

## Interpretation

Rows are territorial context layers only. They are not platform user reports, not veterinary diagnoses, and not individual clinical records in vet.ector. Use them to populate the West Nile layer on the public map.
